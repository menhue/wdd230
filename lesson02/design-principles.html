Visual Hierarchy

www.ikea.com

Visual hierarchy is the principle of arranging elements to show their order of importance. Designers structure visual characteristics—e.g., menu icons—so users can understand information easily. On this site colors are more bright on the Go shopping section, it's easy for the user to find that section.
